About Us

Developed in Chicago, used globally - Our Story

It all started by building static iOS applications for restaurants in and around Chicagoland, more specifically Elmhurst. We moved to Munich Germany for two years and pursued this business. After receiving honest and proactive feedback, we worked and launched with European Restaurants. DISH then turned into a platform where you can (in real time) edit your own restaurant apps without any technical knowledge!


From all over, really!

DISH is currently developed world-wide. With developers and employees working from the United States, Mexico, Brazil, and Germany, we have been able to put together an incredible team to develop this platform; for dynamic restaurant mobile apps.


About DISH

DISH is a product in which restaurant, bar, and convenience store owners can manage, edit, & publish their own apps.
