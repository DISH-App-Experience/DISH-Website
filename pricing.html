FAQ

Take a look at some of the 4 most freuently asked questions when it comes to joining DISH.

  • What is DISH?

    DISH Connect is for restaurant owners who are interested in digitizing their business with mobile apps. With the DISH Connect App, they can get started in the digital restaurant business by managing their menu, implementing a rewards program, obtaining reservations, adding locations, promotions, and more. Using the mobile application strategy attracts a younger, more digital audience to their customer base, and increases business sales.

  • How long does it take to get set up?

    Your restaurant app can be ready in anywhere from 5-7 business days. Everything is set up fairly quickly, although to be on the iOS App Store and Google Play Store, there is a 1-2 business day wait for approval. You can download DISH Connect on the iOS App Store, where you'll manage your app.

  • How does DISH benefit me?

    Not only will DISH provide an outlet for you to connect with customers and grow marketing opportunities, it also calls for customer retention. For restaurants in particular, mobile apps offer a ton of awesome features to boost business in a cost-effective manner.

  • I'm a busy restaurant owner, is this time consuming?

    Clients are able to edit their mobile app on the go, after signing in with their specific credentials. If they are interested in DISH to set up their menu automatically, clients can pay an additional $100 for a launch fee and automatic menu integration.

About DISH

DISH is a product in which restaurant, bar, and convenience store owners can manage, edit, & publish their own apps.
