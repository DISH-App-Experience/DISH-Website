Features

Switch On / Off Features

Not interested in all features? Use the 'Manage Features' section in the Settings screen to switch on the features you think are most useful.

App Customization

Make the look and feel of your app identical to your restaurant in just a few taps! Add custom images, fonts and logos in realtime.

YouTube Demo

Digital Menu

Manage and edit your menu in real time! DISH makes it easy for you to update your menu with little work. No need to contact developers to edit your menu, when you can do it yourself quickly!

YouTube Demo

Rewards Program

Do you have an existing punch-card / rewards program, or looking into implmenting one? DISH's Rewards Program is simple and easy! Each users' punchard is on the app, for each franchised location.

Reservation Requests

Control your busy days with our reservation feature! Accept and deny user submitted requests from users for multiple locations. Reservations include start time, duration, nnumber of seats, name, and more!

About Us Page

Create your custom About Us Page to inform your users about your story, and important contact details!

Events & Promos

Alert your users whenever you have a special event or promotion. This can be used to catching customers attention for your new mobile app! It also drives more retention!

Add Locations

Add your locations in order to secure different locations to scan your card at, give users directions to your nearest location, and assign custom promotions and events to certain locations.

Image Gallery

New restaurant, or looking to show off the interior of your location? Upload it into the Image Gallery, and keep your users updated on whats going on from the tips of their fingers.

Marketing Options!

When checking out, choose from different marketing products for a low cost, set price! Choose from cards, to stickers, and more!

About DISH

DISH is a product in which restaurant, bar, and convenience store owners can manage, edit, & publish their own apps.
